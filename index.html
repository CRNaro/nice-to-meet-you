About Me

  • Background and Introduction: I was born on September 22, 1981, to my parents, Debra and Timothy Naro, and I've spent the majority of my life in the beautiful state of New Hampshire. From an early age, I developed a deep passion for the arts, which eventually led me to pursue a degree in Studio Art at Plymouth State University.
  • Professional Experience: Throughout my career, I've explored various forms of artistic expression. Initially, my love for the arts manifested through visual mediums, but as time went on, I discovered a new artistic outlet—brewing beer. I honed my brewing skills and gained recognition in New Hampshire as a strong and innovative brewer, thanks to my contributions at Throwback Brewery and SoMe Brewing Co.
  • New Horizons: Now, I'm embarking on an exciting new journey that merges my passion for creativity with the fascinating world of computer science and web development. Eager to leave my own creative mark, I've delved into the vast and impressive field of web development, where I'm constantly expanding my knowledge and skills.
  • Mission and Values: My mission is to blend my artistic sensibilities with technical expertise to create visually beautiful and user-friendly websites. I believe that every website should be a unique reflection of its owner's vision and purpose. I'm dedicated to collaorating with clients to b bring their ideas to life while ensuring an engaging user experience.
  • Personal Interests and Hobbies: Beyond my professional life, I enjoy exploring all that life has to offer. Whether it's experimenting with new brewing techniques, immersing myself in the world of art, or seeking inspiration from nature by riding my mountain bike and exploring.
  • Let's Connect: Thank you for visiting my website and learning more about me. I'm excited to connect with fellow artists, entrepreneurs, and people who are passionate about web development. If you have any questions or would like to collaborate on a project, please feel free to reach out to me using the contact information below. Let's bring your vision to life!
